Cookies

Web cookies are small pieces of data stored on the user's computer by the web browser while browsing a website. They are designed to be a reliable mechanism for websites to remember stateful information or to record the user's browsing activity.

  • Advantage: Cookies are used to remember user's information on the pages to remember user's browser activity. They also help in making the browsing experience more efficient.
  • Disadvantage: They can be used to track the browsing activities of the user which can lead to privacy issues.
